Spiritual Reflections

The path to salvation all mortals seek,
And before you lie a million such.
A choice is to be made by thee, O son of man.

Before you stands the path of the enlightened,
Before you, countless ways that claim the truth.
The choice is thine, O sinner,
Choose the road thy soul accepts as true.

To walk with a thousand gods, some have chosen,
To bow in submission, others too,
To deny the name of God, some hearts have chosen,
Yet by divine grace, thou art forgiven and chosen.

Arise, O sinner, clothed in crimson grace,
Walk forth, for thy Lord hath died for thee,
Accept thy salvation, lift thy head high,
For none can claim what the Lord God calls mine.

At the table of gold we shall dine with Him after judgment,
And an eternity of joy our Lord hath promised.
Let my faith not shake nor waver,
For I have chosen to walk with Thee and shall never turn back.
