About Love

Below seven constellations I wonder,
Who made a beauty like thee as I Flander?
In eyes full of perfection I surrender,
May all of heaven see her grandeur.
With her smile, my heart she has plundered,
To say I love her not is a blunder,
Her beauty and glow none can hinder,
Can she ever be mine I ponder.
Delicate she is , like life's ember,
Time after time her smile I remember,
Sweeter than the sweetest of nectar,
Forever my morning star here and after,
An eternity of sunshines I cannot promise,
But an eternity of sun sets I will not miss,
Forever I call you my muse,
Forever I will lay in your hue.
